Describe the most expensive item you have ever bought

You should say:
- When and where you bought it
- What it was
- What you used it for
And explain whether you are glad you bought it

Follow up:
- Do you think people today spend too much money on expensive items?
- Why do some people prefer to buy luxury goods even if they are not necessary?
- How has online shopping influenced people’s purchasing habits, especially when it comes to expensive items?
- What factors influence a person’s decision to buy an expensive product instead of a cheaper alternative?
- Do you think advertisements influence people to buy expensive items?
- In your opinion, is it better to spend money on experiences (like travel) or material possessions (like luxury items)?
