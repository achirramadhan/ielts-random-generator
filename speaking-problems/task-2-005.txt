Describe a photo that is special to you

You should say:
- When and where it was taken
- Who are in the photo
- Why it is special to you
And explain whether you will keep it for a long time

Follow up:
- Who takes photos more often, young people or older people?
- What do young people and old people like to take photos of?
- Why do some people pay a lot of money to hire professional photographers to take photos at some special occasions, such as weddings?
